About Bukidnon

Discover the rich culture and breathtaking landscapes of Bukidnon

The Highland Paradise

Bukidnon is a landlocked province in the Philippines located in the Northern Mindanao region. Known as the "Highland Paradise," it boasts cool climate, vast agricultural lands, and stunning mountain ranges. The province is home to diverse ethnic groups and offers a unique blend of culture and nature.

Kaamulan Festival

Kaamulan is an ethnic cultural festival held annually in Malaybalay City, Bukidnon. It celebrates the customs and traditions of the seven tribal groups that originally inhabited the Bukidnon region.

  • Traditional dances and music performances

  • Indigenous sports competitions

  • Ethnic craft exhibits and food fairs
